    How to Play

    Treezy is a daily puzzle game where you guess the mystery tree using hints. Test your knowledge and share your results!

    Start with the first hint

    Each puzzle begins with Hint 1. Read the clue and look carefully at the image. The hint may describe the tree’s habitat, history, uses, appearance, or identifying features.

    Make your first guess

    Type the name of the tree into the guess box. As you type, Treezy will suggest matching tree species from the list.

    Unlock more hints after each guess

    If your guess is wrong, the next hint unlocks. You can swipe or use the arrows to revisit hints you have already unlocked.

    Use previous guesses to narrow it down

    Your guesses are saved as you play. Green means correct, amber means close, and red means wrong.

    Guess the tree before you run out

    You have 6 guesses. The fewer guesses you need, the better your score.

    Tip: Many tree names include common words like Oak, Maple, Pine, or Cedar. Try different combinations if your first guess is close.
